Our Story

Raj Rai Seafood has been a leading provider of premium ocean delicacies for over a decade. We pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest catch directly from sustainable local fisheries to bring the authentic taste of the sea to your table.

What started as a small family-run dockside operation has grown into a world-class distribution network, yet our heart remains in the deep blue. Every piece of seafood we handle is treated with the utmost respect for the environment and the craft.
